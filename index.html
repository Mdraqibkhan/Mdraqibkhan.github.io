Research Mission

Enhancing Real-Time 3D Perception

My goal is to develop lightweight deep learning models that enhance the accuracy and efficiency of depth estimation systems. By focusing on computational efficiency, my research aims to make high-fidelity 3D reconstruction viable for resource-constrained environments in robotics and immersive technologies.

Application Domains

Research Impact

Autonomous Driving

Improving depth perception for environment sensing and obstacle avoidance in self-driving vehicles. Enabling safer navigation through robust disparity estimation.

AR / VR

Real-time 3D scene understanding for immersive augmented and virtual reality experiences. Lightweight models for mobile AR headsets.

Robotics

Enhancing robotic manipulation and navigation through precise depth sensing. Reducing computational footprint for embedded robotic systems.
